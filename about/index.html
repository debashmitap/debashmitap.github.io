About me

I am a PhD student at the Computer Science Department of Gran Sasso Science Institute (GSSI) in L'Aquila, Italy. Currently, I'm working on the topic of Adaptive Influence Maximization under the supervision of Gianlorenzo D'Angelo and Cosimo Vinci.

About my research

The influence maximization (IM) problem is a well known NP-hard problem of selecting the k most influential nodes in a network. Kempe et al. in their seminal work, "Maximizing the Spread of Influence through a Social Network", formulized the IM problem as a discrete optimization problem. We call this the vanilla/non-adaptive IM problem, which will serve as the base for the adaptive version, which was studied by Golovin and Krause extensively in their paper.
